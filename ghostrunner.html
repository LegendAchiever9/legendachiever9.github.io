Ghostrunner

Played in 2024


Elevator Pitch

Imagine a first person John Wick with a sword parkouring through cyberpunk cityscape levels with some dark synthwave and you have Ghostrunner. This game takes the Titanfall style movement techniques of wall runs and slide jumps and combines it with high octane, one hit-one kill gameplay like Katana Zero.
